Privacy Policy

What we collect

The Good Scroll is designed to be simple and respectful of your privacy. We collect only what we need to operate the app and communicate with you:

  • Email addresses submitted through our waitlist form
  • Basic usage data through Expo and app store analytics (anonymous)
  • Device information required to deliver the app experience

What we don't collect

We do not sell your data. We do not serve ads. We do not track your behavior across other apps or websites. The Good Scroll is built as an antidote to attention-extracting platforms — we have no interest in becoming one.

How we use your email

If you join our waitlist, we use your email address to notify you when the app launches and to share occasional updates about The Good Scroll. You can unsubscribe at any time by replying to any email or contacting us directly at jaredgoodscroll@gmail.com.

Third-party services

We use the following third-party services to operate:

  • Netlify — website hosting and form submissions
  • Pexels — nature photography used as backgrounds
  • Expo — app development and distribution platform
  • Mailchimp — email list management

Each of these services has their own privacy policies governing their use of data.

Data retention

We retain your email address until you request removal. To request deletion of your data, email us at jaredgoodscroll@gmail.com and we will remove it within 30 days.

Children's privacy

The Good Scroll is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify waitlist subscribers of significant changes via email. The effective date at the top of this page reflects the most recent update.

The app

The Good Scroll provides curated Bible verses paired with nature photography in a vertical scroll format. The app is free to download. Premium features may be offered in the future on a subscription basis.

Content

Bible verses presented in The Good Scroll are sourced from public domain translations (KJV) and licensed Bible APIs. Nature photography is sourced from Pexels under their free license. All content is curated for a peaceful, faith-affirming experience.
